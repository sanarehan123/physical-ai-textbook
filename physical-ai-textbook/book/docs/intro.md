# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

This is an AI-native textbook for the Panaversity course.

**Goal**: Teach you how AI moves from software into real robots that walk, talk, and work with humans.

You will learn:
- ROS 2 – The robot operating system
- Gazebo & Unity – Simulate robots
- NVIDIA Isaac Sim – Advanced AI for robots
- Vision-Language-Action (VLA) – Make robots understand voice commands

Made for Panaversity Hackathon – November 2025
