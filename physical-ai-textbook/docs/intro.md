# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook

Welcome to the **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics** textbook, where we explore the intersection of artificial intelligence and physical systems. This comprehensive resource covers the foundations, tools, and applications of embodied intelligence in robotics.

## About This Textbook

This textbook is designed to guide you through the essential concepts of physical AI and humanoid robotics, from foundational principles to advanced implementations. You'll learn about:

- **Module 1**: Foundations of Physical AI & Embodied Intelligence
- **Module 2**: The Robotic Nervous System (ROS 2)
- **Module 3**: Digital Twins & AI-Robot Brains
- **Module 4**: Vision-Language-Action & Capstone Projects

## Getting Started

Begin your journey by exploring the modules in sequence, starting with the fundamentals of physical AI and progressing to advanced topics in humanoid robotics and AI integration.

Each module contains comprehensive chapters with practical examples, code samples, and hands-on exercises to reinforce your learning.
